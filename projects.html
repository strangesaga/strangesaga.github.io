OUT OF ORDER

Play as Detective Thomas as he dives into the most harrowing case of his career. Explore forgotten arcades, piece together key evidence, and survive encounters with things that were never meant to move again.

OUT OF ORDER is a story-driven survival horror fangame inspired by Five Nights at Freddy’s. It blends atmospheric exploration, deduction puzzle-solving, and cinematic storytelling into a mature, psychological thriller.


Key Investigation Features:

  • Advanced investigation board mechanics for connecting clues.
  • Dynamic heart-rate survival system (Hendrix System).
  • Retro VHS aesthetics and immersive analog sound design.
