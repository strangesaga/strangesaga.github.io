OUT OF ORDER

Play as Detective Thomas as he dives into the most harrowing case of his career. Explore forgotten arcades, piece together key evidence, and survive encounters with things that were never meant to move again.

OUT OF ORDER is a story-driven survival horror fangame inspired by Five Nights at Freddy’s. It blends atmospheric exploration, deduction puzzle-solving, and cinematic storytelling into a mature, psychological thriller.


Key Investigation Features:

  • Advanced investigation board mechanics.
  • Dynamic heart-rate survival system (Hendrix System).
  • Retro VHS aesthetics and immersive sound design.

Management / Visual Novel

BREWED MOMENTS

Welcome to Brewed Moments, a cozy slice-of-life management game where every cup tells a story. Step behind the counter of a charming corner cafe and learn the delicate art of brewing the perfect espresso while listening to the lives of your quirky customers.

Manage your ingredients, master new latte art, and create a warm sanctuary for the neighborhood. In this cafe, a good conversation is just as important as a good roast.


Key Features:

  • Deep coffee brewing mechanics and recipe discovery.
  • Branching dialogues with a diverse cast of local patrons.
  • Lo-fi soundtrack to study and relax to.
