OUT OF ORDER

THE DARKNESS NEVER LEFT.

Play as Detective Thomas as he dives into the most harrowing case of his career. Explore forgotten arcades, piece together key evidence, and survive encounters with things that were never meant to move again.

OUT OF ORDER is a story-driven survival horror fangame inspired by Five Nights at Freddy’s. It blends atmospheric exploration, deduction puzzle-solving, and cinematic storytelling into a mature, psychological thriller.

KEY INVESTIGATION MECHANICS

  • Mental Board: Connect clues dynamically to unlock new narrative paths.
  • Hendrix System: A dynamic heart-rate survival system that reacts to terror.
  • Analog Terror: Retro VHS aesthetics and immersive, oppressive sound design.

Management Visual Novel

BREWED MOMENTS

EVERY CUP TELLS A STORY.

Welcome to Brewed Moments, a cozy slice-of-life management game. Step behind the counter of a charming corner cafe and learn the delicate art of brewing the perfect espresso while listening to the lives of your quirky customers.

Manage your ingredients, master new latte art, and create a warm sanctuary for the neighborhood. In this cafe, a good conversation is just as important as a good roast.

COZY FEATURES

  • Brewing Mechanics: Deep coffee crafting and recipe discovery.
  • Narrative Focus: Branching dialogues with a diverse cast of local patrons.
  • Chill Vibes: A gorgeous lo-fi soundtrack to study and relax to.
